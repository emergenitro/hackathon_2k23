How to stay calm

We are all immensely stressed about the self-inflicted situation. However, we must not give up. Try the following strategies to stay calm and composed as we tackle this crisis: (If you are looking for someone to talk to, contact our 24/7 panic hotline.)



  1. De-stressing activities (painting, reading, meditation, gardening, etc)
  2. Drink plenty of water and have regular meals
  3. Talk to your friends or family about the situation
  4. Follow a regular routine

How can you help?

  1. Spread awareness about the crisis
  2. Keep track of updates through our website, and think of ways to face the Aliens- you can contact us with your ideas
  3. Practice gaming to be able to successfully clear their game
  4. Be prepared incase of an invasion!
